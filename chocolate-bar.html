Chocolate Bar

Home  /  Interesting Links  /  GitHub

TLDR: Break chocolate in turns and don't eat the marked piece. Two palyers cut chocolate bar of nxm in turns. One cell is special, player who eats it loses. A turn consists of either breaking off 1 or more rows or 1 or more columns and eating them, or eating the remaining portion of the bar.

Hint:

Reduce this game to another famous math game.

Solution:

If marked cell is a, b, c, d away from the boardeds, game is equivalent to Nim game on 4 piles of sizes a, b, c, d.
